# Demon Slayer Keychain

This project is a keychain made from a combination of Demon Slayer symbols then put onto a PCB. It combines popular features from the anime Demon Slayer, such as Hanafuda earrings, Kokushibo's six eyes, the Metsu symbol, and the Demon Corps logo with wisteria flowers. It has an NFC sticker that will play the Demon Slayer opening when tapped.

## WHY
I am building this because I saw the idea for it on Resolution, and It was something I wanted to try doing because not only would it teach me more about how silkscreens work (I had a lot of unnecessary trouble with silkscreens that could have been avoided if I knew a bit more), but it would also give me a further understanding of how PCBs work internally (i.e the internal layers) and how I can use them to my advantage.
 
## HOW
### BOM
* PCB
* NFC sticker (preferably black)
* keychain

### Fabrication
First, Head over to JLCPCB and upload the gerber zip file, it will autofill most of the specifications.
Make sure to select **black** for the PCB color, otherwise the back of the PCB will not look good.
Once the PCB and parts arrive, attach the Keychain through the premade hole using pliers.
Then, unpeel the NFC sticker and attach it to the back (side without design) of the PCB.
Download an app such as NFC tools on your device, click 'write,' then 'url.'
Upload the following link: https://youtu.be/pmanD_s7G3U?si=PfzA8vtHQEHkoVT8&t=59 _this will start the song at 59 seconds (my favorite part), but if you want to change the start time go for it!_
Click "paste" and "ok," then click "write" and scan the NFC to your device.

### USE
Attach the keychain to your keyring and the PCB will be ready to look amazing!
